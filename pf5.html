    • 05. Merivale Fish Market

    Logo Design & Branding

    Merivale Fish Market & Seafood Grill has been a part of the Ottawa dining scene for over 20 years; a family-run local store and restaurant which, according to their customers, takes pride in running their business with passion and freshness.

    In rebranding their logo for a school project, my aim was to create a mark that would not just be a fresh face for the locale, but that would consolidate their brand into a look that could be carried through, consistently, in both restaurant and store. In keeping with their old logo, the new brand features a friendly little fish character, with a strong iconic look, but still with a rustic appeal. The idea is that this logo can shine in a modern website, but also be carved into a wooden sign above the door.
