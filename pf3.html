    • 03. Frontcrush

    Branding · UX · UI · Copywriting

    When a web developer friend from Lima, Perú, came to us for help creating an identity and website for his new business, we knew we had to give him something we would be proud to don ourselves. While portraying a sober and professional look, we still wanted to make sure we transmitted his friendly and hard-working personality. To do this, we created an energetic mark to match his bold name: Frontcrush, and coupled it with bright colours and playful illustrations. The essence of his personality comes together in the content. Full of useful data regarding web design and current e-commerce trends, it is meant to be an access point for his customers, to inform them first, and do business with them second.
