    • 04. Multiplica tus Clientes

    UX · UI · Copywriting · Illustration

    Multiplica tus Clientes is a Peruvian project that focuses on helping e-commerce businesses through improved website structures, SEO, and online campaigns. In need of a friendly and trustworthy identity, we decided to give the brand a voice through a character inspired by retro store fronts, that walks clients through the services and benefits the company provides. In love with their new partner, the company named him Wilson, in honour of the main street in the tech district of Lima.
