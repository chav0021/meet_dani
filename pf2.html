    • 02. Explorandes

    UX · UI · Copywriting

    Explorandes is a Peruvian adventure travel company that has been sharing life changing experiences with visitors to Peru since 1975. Our priority when redesigning their website was to speak volumes of the level of expertise, the pioneering spirit, and the excellent and honest quality of work that has characterized them through their almost 40 years of service.

    We aimed to make the website more than just a basic portal of information; an exciting and interactive experience, as thrilling as traveling with the Explorandes team would actually be. The site is meant to be a preview into the experience, offering detailed insight to their service as well as beauty of the country, while sharing plenty of useful and engaging information and resulting in the easy booking of a tour.
