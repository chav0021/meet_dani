    • 01. Changing the Face of Privacy

    Concept · Photography · Typesetting

    Although one of my older projects, this spread — designed in second year Typography class — is still one of my favorites. The article touches on the roll that technology has played in transorming our personal boundaries, addressing the turning of our private information into profit by large corporations. It was this 'defacing' and packaging that I chose to base my art direction on, alluding to the commercialization of our information, and the consequences this can have in how we identify as people.
