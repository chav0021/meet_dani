    • 01. Changing the Face of Privacy

    Concept · Photography · Typesetting

    Although one of my older projects, this spread — designed in second year Typography class — is still one of my favorites. The article touches on the roll that technology has played in transorming our personal boundaries, addressing the issue of large corporations turning in our data for profit. In this scenario, our identities — the things that make us, us — become nothing more than a neatly packaged product. It was this 'defacing' that I chose to steer the art direction for this piece on, playing with stark colours, sharp angles, and one hell of a creepy image.
