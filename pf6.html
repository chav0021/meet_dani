    • 06. Motel Montcalm

    Branding · Photography · Web Development

    The proud new owners of Gatineau-based Motel Montcalm came to Jared and I with a mission: give the Motel a facelift. The rebrand had to reflect the fresh approach the new management was bringing to the table. Brand new rooms, improved facilities, comfortable space, and a stylish environment for singles and families alike.

    The clean and geometric mark gave the motel a new and flexible brand to work with. It speaks of the rolling Gatineau Hills behind the locale, and shines a light on the quality of stay one can expect at the motel. We were also lucky enough to help them photograph and build their (and our) very first website, which you can see here.
