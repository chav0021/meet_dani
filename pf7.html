    • 07. USIL Websites

    UX · UI · Creative direction

    USIL presented us with a unique challenge. Working with a pre-existing brand and rigorous guidelines, we were asked to create 5 separate websites for the five institutions the university holds. Each had their own target market and each needed their own look — but they all had to tie in to the USIL identity.

    We did this with a consistent structure but multiple personalities, each in their own way friendly and easy to navigate, making access to the large amount of information this 20-year-old institution holds straightforward and engaging.
