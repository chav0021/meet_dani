    • 07. USIL Websites

    UX · UI · Creative direction

    The Universidad San Ignacio de Loyola, in Lima, Perú, presented us with a unique challenge. Working with a pre-existing brand and rigorous guidelines, we were asked to create five separate websites, for the five institutions the university holds: the main University, an academy, a school for chefs, a locale in Miami, and an elementary-through-high-school institution. Each had their own target market and each needed their own look — but they all had to tie in to the USIL identity.

    We did this with a consistent structure, easy to navigate intuitively. Our aim was to make access to the large amount of information this 20-year-old institution holds as straightforward and engaging as possible. Each site is infused with its own distinctive personality; professional, friendly and speaking closely to their audience.
